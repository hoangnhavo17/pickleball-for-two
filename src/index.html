Pickleball rules (singles)

Scoring

  • Score call: server's score, then receiver's (HUD).
  • Points: only the server scores; receiver wins the rally → side-out.
  • Game: first to 11, win by 2.

Court zones

  • Kitchen (NVZ): red strip at the net (7 paddle-units).
  • Box: kitchen line to baseline (15 paddle-units).
  • Runback: behind the baseline before you serve.

Serve & two-bounce

  • Angle 1–4, then Space on the power bar.
  • Two-bounce: serve bounces on receiver's side; return bounces on server's side. No volleys until both (HUD).
  • Serve fault: first bounce in opponent's kitchen.

Kitchen (NVZ)

  • You may stand in the kitchen.
  • Fault: volley in the kitchen before the ball bounces on your side.

Rally

  • Volleys OK after two-bounce rule (not in kitchen before bounce).
  • Second bounce on your side → point lost.
  • Must clear the net and land in bounds.

Shots

  • Height: high smash, mid drive, low lob.
  • Q soft: kitchen → dink; box → drop.
